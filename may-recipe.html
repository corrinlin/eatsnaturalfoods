May Recipe of the Month

Hot and Smoky Rigatoni Bake

Total time: 35 minutes
Prep time: 25 minutes
Serves: 6-8
Adapted from Trader Joe's: http://traderjoes.com/Recipes/recipe/479

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Rigatoni Pasta
  • 1 lb. Italian Sausage, casing removed
  • 2 tsp. Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 2 tsp. Smoked Paprika
  • 45 oz. Tomato Basil Marinara (about 1-3/4 jars)
  • 1 cup whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan, divided
  • 2 cups grated smoke mozzarella chese
  • fresh basil, shredded, for garnish (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350℉. Bring a pot of water to boil. Add rigatoni to pot and boil for 10 minutes, ust until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. While pasta is cooking, in a small bowl, combine ricotta and half of the grated parmesan. Stir and set aside. In a large sauce pan over medium heat, add olive oil and sausage. Brown sasuage for 7-9 minutes until it reaches 145℉, breaking meat up with a spoon as it cooks. Add paprika and stir to combine. Reduce heat to low and add marinara. Heat until sauce is warmed through, stirring occasionally.
  3. Return drained pasta to pot and add half of marinara mixture, stirring to combine. Gently fold in ricotta mixture. Transfer half of the pasta to a 9x13 pan. Sprinkle half of the smoked mozzarella over pasta. Top with remaining pasta and remaining sauce and spread in an even layer. Top pasta with remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheeses.
  4. Place pan in middle rack of oven and bake until edges are bubbling and cheese is melted, about 35 minutes. Remove pan from oven and let cool for 10 minutes. Garnish with shredded basil if desired, and serve.
