April Recipe of the Month

Roasted Salmon with Crispy Potatoes and Broccoli

Total time: 30 minutes
Prep time: 20 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 head broccoli
  • 1 lb. gold potatoes
  • 1 large red onion
  • 3 tbsp. olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • pepper
  • 1-1/2 lb skinless salmon fillet
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 clove garlic

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 450℉. On a rimmed baking sheet, toss together the broccoli, potatoes, and online with the oil, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper. Spread in an even layer and roast for 15 minutes.
  2. Season the salmon with 1/4 teaspoons each salt and pepper, nestle it among the vegetables and continue roasting until the vegetables are golden brown and tender and the salmon is opaque throughout, 8 to 10 more minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise, lemon juice, and garlic. Serve with the fish and the vegetables.
