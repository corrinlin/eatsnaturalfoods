March Recipe of the Month

Cumin-y Riced Cauliflower

Cooking time: 12 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon organic extra virgin coconut oil
  • 1 cup diced onions
  • 1 bag fresh riced cauliflower
  • 1-2 tsp cumin to taste
  • salt

Directions

  1. In a alrge skillet, heat coconut oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add diced onions and sauté 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add riced cauliflower to skillet and sauté another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add cumin and sauté another two minutes, until fragrant.
  5. Season to taste with salt and serve immediately.
